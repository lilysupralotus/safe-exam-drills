Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

The Underwriter's Drill ("the app") does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

What the app does on your device

All quiz questions, your answers, and your scores are kept only in your device's memory while the app is open. Nothing is sent to a server, and nothing is saved permanently once you close the app, unless a future version explicitly states otherwise on this page.

Third parties

The app loads typefaces from Google Fonts when online. Google may log standard technical request data (such as IP address) as part of serving those font files, under Google's own privacy policy. The app itself does not add any tracking, analytics, or advertising code.

Payments

If you purchased this app, that transaction was handled entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment details — Google's own privacy policy governs that transaction.

Children

The app is study material for an adult professional licensing exam and is not directed at children.

Changes to this policy

If this app ever begins collecting data, this page will be updated first, with a new "last updated" date above.
